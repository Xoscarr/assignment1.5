Biography

I was born and raised in San Diego; I am 26 years old. I am the youngest of 2 older sisters. I have a Pitbull named Maximus he is 15 years old. I am really interested in photography, drawing, and digital art. I consider myself a creative person and an artist. i have been drawing since I was young, and I have made it a point to continue to expand those skills. I believe it’s necessary to express yourself, I think it’s good for the soul.

I look forward to learning how to develop software and how to use it in a way to benefit others. I am really inspired by the concept of using technology to enhance one’s day to life or overall experience. it is a goal of mine to develop software to help someone with a disability or illness. I also look forward to using coding to create just in general and add it under my belt in things enjoy designing.
